Caravan:

  • The point of the game is to get your 3 "caravans" from values 21 to 26, with the higher value being better.
  • You cannot duplicate cards from the same "set", but can theoretically have four-of-a-kind (or rank) from each casinos if you wanted (4x 10s from The Tops, Ultra-Luxe, Gomorrah, Lucky 38, Vikki & Vance, Sierra Madre, totalling 24 cards).
  • You can both build you deck before games and discard cards from your hand without losing a term before your first caravan starts.
